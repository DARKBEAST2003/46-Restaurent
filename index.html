Our Story

Crafting Culinary Excellence Since 1995

LuxeBistro was founded with a simple vision: to create a dining experience that combines exceptional food, impeccable service, and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Our executive chef, Antoine Moreau, brings over 25 years of international experience to our kitchen, crafting dishes that blend classic techniques with modern innovation.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and producers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability, ensuring that every dish tells a story of flavor and care.
